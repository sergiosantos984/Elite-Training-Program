About Us


Who are we

We are The Elite Training Program based in Estoril Praia Stadium in Lisbon, Portugal

What we do

We deliver a fun and effective program that offers 2 training sessions per day, our coaches are both qualified and enthusiastic creating the perfect environment to develop! Our program runs in weekdays, from Monday to Friday , the training session are delivered at 8:30am and 7:30pm (subject to change).

In our daily training, the program delivers smaller group sessions to develop specific skills (i.e.technical training) to enhance their football ability .

So...its just that?

No... we also offer other activities such as surfing, trampoline , hiking and cycling . Some are included in the price, yet, some require additional costs.

We run on a fixed schedule that is subject to change on a weekly basis, thus, when enrolling onto the program, the candidates are subject to the already planned activities for the duration of their stay.

Whats our goal

More than just developing your football ability, we also prioritise the physical devlopment that is so important in football, thus, we have a personal trainer in every session to maximise the physical development during your stay with us !
